Resources



If you don't understand how to do one of the steps outlined in the checklist below, please see the FAQ page to see if your question is there.
  • Join the Randomizer Discord
  • Sign up for the Tournament Pings in the #rules channel. I will be using this ping to update tournament rules and explanations. (If you really want to you can untick this after Fresh Faces).
  • Have the randomizer setup to the 3.0 (v2) generator and 2.0.0 OpenKH Mod Manager. (details for updating if you already had the randomizer insalled can be found in #announcments in the randomizer discord).
  • Add the rando4ukraine4 preset into your presets folder within your generator.
  • Have livesplit downloaded.
  • Get the loadless timer installed into your livesplit.
  • Sign up for a racetime.gg account.
  • Connect your twitch to your racetime.gg account.
  • Create a layout that has your tracker, gameplay, and timers visible.
  • (Optional) Sign up for Active Racer in the #rules channel since we have people who like to causally race these settings for practice.





The randomizer community has amazing setup guides on their homepage and the wonderful CoreySG9 made a video guide to help newcomers get setup. Additionally, check our calendar for community events where the randomizer community hosts practice races and personalized setup sessions!
