A Message to the Racers

Whether you are participating to compete, to contribute to charity, or becuase I begged asked you to join, thank you. From the bottom of my heart, it means a lot to me. Your contribution is amazing and I am very excited to include you in this very special event. The goal of this tournament is to bring Kingdom Hearts and randomizer enthusiasts alike who are still in the beginner stages of their KH2 randomizer experience and raise money for a cause that is near and dear to me personally. Feel free to use the navigation bar at the top of the page to see our FAQ and resources for beginners! See below for more information about the charity we are raising money for: Project Hope for the Ukraine Crisis. See the RSVP Form Tab to submit to us your interest in participating. The more sign ups we get the more money we can raise for Project Hope!

About the Cause

The nation of Ukraine and its people is an incredibly beautiful and integral part of our world. Unfortunately, this beauty is being massacred and the beautiful photos you see all around this site that make Urkaine shine is now painted with death and destruction. If you are unfamiliar with the Ukraine Crisis, listed below are some links (and images) of the extent of the damage caused by the war. Hopefully, by working with Project Hope's efforts to provide as much humanitarian aid as possible to Ukranian victims, we can play a small (but very important) role in rebuilding what Russia's invaders destroyed.
