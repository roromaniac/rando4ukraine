Donation Incentive List


We plan to have MANY incentives to reward our generous donors and viewers. These incentives are meant to showcase that Kingdom Hearts can be played in ways you might have never imagined. A few of the planned events are detailed below. If you have incentive ideas (even if they are not KH related), feel free to let me know! I'm open to all options.

$1000 Incentive: Nightmare Settings Race (DoubleADewi vs. Ragnoth)

Have you ever wanted to start playing Kingdom Hearts again with no tools or movement to work with and no willingness to live because of it? Yeah, me neither. However, for some reason, our resident nightmare correspondents DoubleADewi and Ragnoth just keep coming back for more of this. Get ready to see superbosses having important magic spells, second chance and once more being hidden behind Absent Silhouettes, and other absurd randomizations. This will require our racers to do an insane amount of content with very few tools and requires an insane amount of talent (and patience).



$2000 Incentive: Chaos Modded Randomizer Race ( subconjazz vs. Marsul )

You may think you have seen everything when it comes to modded versions of Kingdom Hearts, but serial modder TheNja09 might give you a run for your money. He constantly tries to make the randomizer more chaotic entertaining for viewers and players alike. For this race, he will choose a select number of mods that change... just about everything. We'll see how the racers fare on the tournament settings when the gameplay gets redefined.



$2500 Incentive: Bring Back Buffed Beginners (WallpeSH vs. SuperStarEarth vs. LittleHerdez vs. SwiftShadow)

People who were once entrants into these beginner tournaments are now holding their own against regulars. To see their progress we brought back the grand finalists of SeriouslySurly's last two beginner tournaments to compete in a 4 person race to get All Blue Numbers (ABN).



$3000 Incentive: Boss Enemy Lockout Race (o0DemonBoy0o (DA) vs. Zeddikus)

Did you know even the bosses can be randomized? For this event 2 racers will go head to head trying to find a list of 25 bosses. Once one racer finds and defeats a boss, the other player will NOT be able to claim that boss. Whoever ends with the most bosses wins. Our racers DA and Zeddikus are boss enemy experts but these constraints will definitely throw some wrenches in their plans.



$4000 Incentive: My Friends Are My Power Randomizer Run (              &              vs.              &              )

Inspired by Regular Pat, this incentive requires 2 pairs of racers. Why? Because in a "My Friends Are My Power" run, one person is blindfolded while the other person gives directions about where to go. We will set a timer for 2 hours (subject to change) and the team with the most important checks found when the timer expires wins!



$5000 Incentive: The Kingdom Hearts II Drinking Game Coop (roromaniac8 and Metthaios)

This coop will happen at the very end of the charity tournament to celebrate the event's success (hopefully). The current plan is to allow users to come up with 25 Kingdom Hearts(ish) words that the racers cannot say. If they do, they must drink.



Bounty Incentive List

What are these? These are for anyone in the community that wants to place a donation bounty on something happening. A few examples up for grabs are displayed below. If you have your own bounty idea in mind, let me know and I will update this page to reflect your bounty.

  • Donate $X if someone get more than 75% of the possible points in the bracket prediction.
  • Donate $X if a pair of racers go berserk (turn TWTNW on).
  • Donate $X if a race starts with a proof.
