April 1

This is the main qualifier that ALL racers will participate in. Upon completion of the race, we will divide those who finished outside of the Top 32 in half. The bottom half will unfortunately be eliminated. The top half, will compete against the same number of people that make up the bottom of the top 32. Simply show up to the racetime having completed the Racer Checklist for this one. If you cannot make it on April 1 7pm EST, please sign up for an async by DMing one of the TOs.

April 6

The chopping block is reserved for the middle of the pack and half the racers will claim their spot in the Top 32. The other half will be eliminated. You will be tagged after the race on April 1 in the #tourney-discussions channel if you need to do the Chopping Block qualifier. You can either do the race live on April 6 7pm EST or async anytime between April 2 and April 5 by DMing one of the TOs.

April 7 and Onwards

By April 7, the Top 32 will have been decided, and we will have a bracket for the remainder of the tournament. - Challonge - Tourney Discussions
