April 1



This is the main qualifier that ALL racers will participate in. Upon completion of the race, we will divide those who finished outside of the Top 32 in half. The bottom half will unfortunately be eliminated. The top half, will compete against the same number of people that make up the bottom of the top 32. Simply show up to the racetime having completed the Racer Checklist for this one. If you cannot make it on April 1 7pm EST, please sign up for an async by DMing one of the TOs. The graphic below hopefully clarifies what the outcome of April 1 looks like. We decided to go with this (admittedly more confusing) structure because it allows for more people to race at least twice while also incentivizing people to finish as fast as possible on April 1.

April 6



The chopping block is reserved for the middle of the pack and half the racers will claim their spot in the Top 32. The other half will be eliminated. You will be tagged after the race on April 1 in the #tourney-discussions channel in the randomizer discord if you need to do the Chopping Block qualifier. You can either do the race live on April 6 7pm EST or async anytime between April 2 and April 5 by DMing one of the TOs.



April 7 and Onwards



By April 7, the Top 32 will have been decided, and we will have a bracket for the remainder of the tournament. We will create a publicly viewable bracket on Challonge where all the matchups and updates will be posted. If you make it to the Top 32, you must DM your opponent and find an appropriate time to race within the timeframe of the tournament. Upon agreeing to a time, navigate to #tourney-discussions and ping roromaniac8#2464 with both racer names and the date/time in EST you have agreed upon.
